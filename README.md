## ✏️ Customize

**Format Explanation:**
```
"YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SS"
 ↓    ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓
 Year Mo Day Hr Min Sec
```

- **YYYY** = 4-digit year (2025, 2026, etc.)
- **MM** = 2-digit month (01=Jan, 02=Feb, ... 12=Dec)
- **DD** = 2-digit day (01 to 31)
- **T** = Separator (keep this!)
- **HH:MM:SS** = Time in 24-hour format
